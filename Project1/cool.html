COOL CAPTAIN


Mahendra Singh Dhoni( born July 7,1981) is an Indian professional cricketer. He was captain of the Indian national team in limited-overs formats from 2007 - 2017 and in Test cricket from 2008-2014.
Dhoni is widely considered one of the greatest cricket captains, wicket-keeper-batsman and finishers in the history of cricket. He plays as a right-handed wicket-keeper-batsman and is known for his calm captaincy and his ability to finish matches in tight situations.
He is also the current captain of Chennai Super Kings in the Indian Premier League.

Achievements

Dhoni holds an honorary rank of Lieutenant Colonel in the Parachute Regiment of the Indian Territorial Army.
The honorary rank was presented to him by the Indian Army in 2011 for his service to the nation as a cricketer.
After completing five parachute training jumps from Indian Army aircraft in the Agra training camp, he became a qualified paratrooper in 2011
In August 2019 he completed a two-week stint with the Territorial Army in Jammu and Kashmir
